Entrepreneur Speaking

Andy moved to the United States from Milan, Italy as a student athlete at UC Santa Cruz. Shortly after college he began his career at Panda Express Restaurant in Palo Alto, CA. Passionate about food and fitness he had his first entrepreneurship stint in 2015 as a food truck owner in Silicon Valley. Soon after, his company evolved into an online fitness food delivery service for athletes, MealPro. Today andy is the venture backed CEO of MealPro, he holds a B.A. in Economics from UC Santa Cruz and is an advocate for entrepreneurship in everyday life. In his free time Andy enjoys speaking at schools to educate about entreprneurship and enjoys mentoring student lead startups in B2C eCommerce food or fitness space in his startup mentoring program.
